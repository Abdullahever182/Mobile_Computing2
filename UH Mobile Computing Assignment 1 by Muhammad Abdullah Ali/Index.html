Goal

The ultimate purpose of E-Reader is to make the reading experience for E-book readers as comfortable, engaging & convenient as possible. The primary users of this app shall be E-book users who would like to immediately download & read novels on their Mobiles phones or Tablets instead of having to buy & wait for a paperback of the book to be delivered. With this app, your Android device functions as an ePub reader as it takes care of.epub files you've obtained from the web and makes them accessible for reading as a book. This app shall support numerous formats e.g. Epub, PDF, Word, Mobi, COMIC etc allowing users to read their novels, word documents, comics, magazines & journals on this app rather than having to download an Ebook Reader or Comic Book App that supports only one or two formats. For users that find the screen too bright & straining on the eyes they can switch to the E-Readers night mode feature which darkens the background while lightening the text to mmake the user reading experience more comfortable. It arranges the books in a list based on the order of users latest reads & saves their reading progress allowing the users to resume from where they left off instead of needing to browse through the pages. The app has a search feature allowing users to browse which ever book they want to read from their collection instead of having to scroll down the list. For users who's native language is not English they need not worry as this app supports Ebooks of all languages. It enables users to create collections for sorting their books. Lastly it allows easy navigation inside the book as users can turn pages with a tap or swipe & features a search engine to search for text inside books.
